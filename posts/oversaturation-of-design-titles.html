Oversaturation of design titles

Dec 10, 2022  — 
Design

Design-ism: A word or phrase designers use that most people don’t understand.

Confusion #

Below are a few titles. It's easy to say how oversaturated titles have become within the design industry. Every year it gets worse. As of 2022: UX (User Experience) Designer, UI (User Interface) Designer, Visual Designer, Interaction Designer (IxD), Product Designer, Information Designer, Graphic Designer, Voice Experience Designer (VX), AR/VR/MR/AI Designer, Web Designer, Experiential Designer, Game Designer, Art Director, Creative Director, Junior/Senior Creative, Creative Lead, Design Lead, Motion Designer, Front End-Designer, Multi-media Designer, Chief Creative Officer, Head of Design, Head of Creative, Design Researcher, UX Researcher, UX Developer, Copy Writing, UX Writing, Creative Executive, VP of Design, VP Creative, Associate Creative Director, Design Director, Design Manager, Senior Designer, Brand Designer. Visit the site below to view a satirical title generator for designers seeking a title apart from other designers. https://designtitles.com

You get the gist #

Not everyone we communicate with will have a background in design or be familiar with design terminology. By using language that is accessible to all, we create a more inclusive and welcoming environment for collaboration and communication.
